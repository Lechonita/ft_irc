- un nickname par client, de maximum 9 caracteres
- Channels names are strings (beginning with a '&' or '#' character) of
   length up to 200 characters.  Apart from the the requirement that the
   first character being either '&' or '#'; the only restriction on a
   channel name is that it may not contain any spaces (' '), a control G
   (^G or ASCII 7), or a comma (',' which is used as a list item
   separator by the protocol).
- the client that creates a channel becomes an operator of this channel
- a command can only take up to 15 parameters (that's a lot)
- 512 characters (including \r\n, so really 510) maximum per messages

LISTE DES COMMANDES

CHANNEL COMMANDS :
	- JOIN (join channel)
	- PART (leave channel)
	- NAMES (list les utilisateurs de la channel)

 BASIC COMMANDS :
	x PASS (pour rentrer le mdp)
	x NICK (change your nick)
	x USER (set username and mode)
	- QUIT (quit server)
	- LIST (list channels)
	- PRIVMSG (send a private message to another user)
	- PING (PONG)
	- WHOIS (get personnal information about a user)

CHANNEL OPERATOR COMMANDS :
	- KICK
	- INVITE
	- TOPIC
	- MODE
